Screenwriting

My very first class in college was an Intro to Screenwriting course and I fell in love with it instantly. I read the textbook on formatting scripts in two days because I found it fascinating. It wasn't until my TV Screenwriting course that I realized this is something I excelled at and could pursue as a future career. My preferred area of screenwriting is Sit Coms.

Abbott Elementary Spec Script

The Abbott Elementary gang gets snowed in after the school district doesn't call a snow day. A Phanatic religion, non FDA-approved treats, a "Here's Johnny" moment, and other shenanigans ensue as the teachers try to keep the kids under control while figuring out how to get them home safely.
